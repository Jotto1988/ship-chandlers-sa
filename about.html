Our Story

Since the city's establishment as a vital victualing station in 1652 for ships traversing to and from the Dutch East Indies, the Port of Cape Town has continuously grown and progressed. Established in 1994 (as Express Ship Chandlers), we are built on a foundation of expertise and knowledge.

Our Mission

To make ship chandling smoother, quicker, and more environmentally responsible while protecting the vital waters we all depend on.

Our Vision

To help shape a better future for maritime supply, where reliable service and responsible choices are always intertwined.
