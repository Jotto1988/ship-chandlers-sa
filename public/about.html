Our Story

Dedicated to Maritime Excellence in Cape Town

Ship Chandlers South Africa is proud to serve the maritime industry from its strategic location in the historic Cape Town Harbour[cite: 57].

Since the city's establishment as a vital victualing station in 1652 for ships traversing to and from the Dutch East Indies, the Port of Cape Town has continuously grown and progressed[cite: 58].
