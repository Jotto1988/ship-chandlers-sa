Our Story: Dedicated to Maritime Excellence

Ship Chandlers South Africa is proud to serve the maritime industry from its strategic location in the historic Cape Town Harbour.

Since the city's establishment as a vital victualing station in 1652, the Port of Cape Town has continuously grown. Today, it is a bustling hub situated along one of the world's busiest trade routes.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to make ship chandling smoother, quicker, and more environmentally responsible. We aspire to help shape a better future for maritime supply, where reliable service and responsible choices are always intertwined.

Proven Reliability

Established in 1994 (originally as Express Ship Chandlers), we have built a foundation of expertise that enables us to consistently exceed client expectations with unbeatable pricing and round-the-clock assistance.
